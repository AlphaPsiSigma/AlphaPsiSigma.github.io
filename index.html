Dr. Jimmy Y. Zhong (Dr. JZ)

Founding Director

Cognitive Neuroscientist & Psychologist

Dr. Jimmy Y. Zhong is a distinguished Cognitive Neuroscientist and Psychologist whose work sits at the intersection of science, education, and innovation. He obtained his PhD in Cognitive Neuroscience, Cognitive Aging, and Quantitative Psychology (Highest Distinction) from Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, USA, in 2019. Prior to that, he earned both his Bachelor's and Master's degrees from the National University of Singapore (NUS), Singapore. He graduated as a top scholar at both institutions.

Academically, Dr. JZ has broad and interdisciplinary interests, spanning across Spatial Cognition and Navigation, Cognitive Aging, the Neuroscience of Consciousness, Applied Psychology, and Artificial Intelligence (AI).

As the Founder of Alpha Psi Sigma, Dr. Zhong is channeling his extensive academic expertise and professional experience into initiatives aimed at benefiting parents and their children. By integrating insights from neuroscience, psychology, and AI, he is developing educational strategies and paradigms that promote critical thinking, cognitive development, and experiential learning. His vision is to empower families with the tools and knowledge needed to thrive in an increasingly complex and AI-driven world.

Sim Kuan GOH, PhD

Founding Director

AI & Brain Science Researcher · IEEE Senior Member

What happens when you put a world-class AI researcher and a brain scientist in the same room? You get Sim Kuan Goh — and frankly, the future of education looks a lot more exciting because of it.

Sim Kuan holds a B.Eng. and PhD from the National University of Singapore (NUS), and is currently an Assistant Professor at Xiamen University Malaysia. His research sits at the electrifying crossroads of Artificial Intelligence and Brain Science — exploring how machines can learn from the mind, and how the mind can be better understood through machines.

An IEEE Senior Member, Sim Kuan has presented and published his work at some of the most prestigious venues in the world, including NeurIPS, ACL, and EMNLP — conferences where the brightest minds in AI gather to shape the direction of the field. In short, he is not just following where technology is going; he is helping decide.

At Alpha Psi Sigma, Sim Kuan brings this deep technical expertise to a mission that is deeply human: helping children learn better, think sharper, and grow with confidence in an AI-driven world.

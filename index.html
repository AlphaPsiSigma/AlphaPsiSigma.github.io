Alpha Psi Sigma Online Educational Consultation Services

What do we do?

At Alpha Psi Sigma, we are a team of professional educators providing busy parents with the practical tools and mindset to manage and discipline their child(ren) for real-world success. Our services are targeted at parents who are especially concerned about their child(ren) underperforming and failing in school and who are clueless about remedial approaches. We are here to teach such parents about the best solutions and the road forward for them and for their child(ren).

How much do we charge?

For Singapore- and Malaysia-born parents residing in Singapore or Malaysia:

Online consultation rate: SG $2 per minute
Surcharge for more than one child: SG $60
Advance GST booking charge (per session): SG $20
Hourly rate (1 child): SG $140
Hourly rate (2+ children): SG $200

For parents born outside Singapore and Malaysia (residing anywhere):

Online consultation rate: US $3 per minute
Surcharge for more than one child: US $60
Advance GST booking charge (per session): US $20
Hourly rate (1 child): US $200
Hourly rate (2+ children): US $260

Offline intelligence gathering & research services:

SG/MY-born parents residing in SG/MY: SG $1 per minute per child
All other parents: US $1 per minute per child

How do I pay?

Parents residing in Singapore: DBS PayLah or PayNow (directed to your consultant).
Parents residing outside Singapore (including Malaysia): PayPal (directed to your consultant). If you do not have a PayPal account, you can easily establish one for online transactions.

Why is the pricing set at this level?

Our pricing is pegged to the hourly wage of an adjunct university lecturer/professor in Singapore and is non-expensive. Our prices are below the median market rate for compensating full-time university lecturers/professors in Singapore, and are also cheaper than what other consultancies will offer you.

Are there discounts available?

Yes — loyal customers who have consulted us at least three times are eligible for discounts. The discount amount is negotiable between the loyal customer and the consultant and will be decided on a case-by-case basis.

How do I book a slot?

Click on "Book a Slot" on our website. Send us a message with your request and any additional queries and we will get back to you through messaging services (e.g., WhatsApp, SMS) on your phone.

All pricing is subject to change. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of current rates.
